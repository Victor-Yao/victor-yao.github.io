## Collect and compare Internet Explorer settings

1. Download and run [IEDigest](https://aka.ms/IEDigest).

2. Select **Create Report**.

    ![start tracing](/assets/images/iedigest1.jpg)

3. Verify the `IEDigest` folder is created at your desktop.
