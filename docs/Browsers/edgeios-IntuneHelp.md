## Collect Intune help data from Edge on iOS

1. Open Microsoft Edge, reproduce the issue.

2. Go to `edge://intunehelp` and tap **Get Started**.

    ![Get Started](/assets/images/edgeios-intunehelp1.jpg)

2. Copy the **Reference ID**. Then tap **Share Logs** and save the logs locally.

    ![Save log](/assets/images/edgeios-intunehelp2.jpg)
